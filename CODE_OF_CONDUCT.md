# Code of Conduct

This project follows a respectful, inclusive collaboration model.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent, even during disagreement.
- Focus feedback on code and ideas, never on people.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation.
- Harassment in any form.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive trolling or repeated bad-faith interactions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban
participants who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately through the channel listed in `SECURITY.md`.
